Welcome to Nfolio a new project currently under development by Nick Lewis with contributions from a small bunch of international photographers. If you are a fellow geek, you may like to know that this project is built with AngularJS, backed by Firebase for real-time data and hosting (we do also use Digital Ocean), images stored on Cloudinary for super-fast image retrieval. If you are a fellow photographer and interested in joining the project, please follow @nicklewis on Twitter for news and the #nfolio hashtag.
